Description

A straightforward recipe for the succulent specialty of Saint-Etienne.

Ingredients

  • A lot of potatoes
  • 200g of high quality bacon bits
  • Bonus : Eggs, cheese, anything you like..

Steps

  1. Peel and grate the potatoes
  2. Mix the grated potatoes with the bacon bits and any other bonus ingredients
  3. Cook the mix by spreading it on a pan (don't make it too thick !)
  4. Enjoy this delicious meal !
