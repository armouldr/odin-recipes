Crepes

Description

The simplest recipe for scrumptious crepes that will make your jaw drop.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 liter of milk
  • 250g of flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of sunflower oil
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together with the same order as the list
  2. Let the mix rest during 1 hour or 2
  3. Cook the crepes using a flat pan
