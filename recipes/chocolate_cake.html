Crepes

Description

A good chocolate cake.

Ingredients

  • 200g of dark chocolate
  • 100g of butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 50g of flour
  • 100g of sugar

Steps

  1. Melt the chocolate and butter in a pan
  2. Mix the sugar, eggs, and flour together and add the chocolate and butter
  3. Cook it in the oven for approximately 20 minutes
